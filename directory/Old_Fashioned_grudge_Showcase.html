“An old fashioned Grudge” is a Denver 48 Film Fest submission.

Directed by Sean Heraty II and Austin Anderson, “An Old Fashioned Grudge” tells a story about a recently retired detective [Frank Oden] not being contempt without doing his work. that is until his daughter [Amanda Edwards] alerting him that his granddaughters have been kidnapped. now picking back up the hat he must solve this case, served old fashioned.

An old fashioned grudge following the rules of the Denver 48 film fest, was given a genre, a character, a prop, and a line of dialogue. the production team has to include all of these into their film in order to compete. the prompts were as stated
Genre: Noir
Character: Zeke or Zena Westwater, bartender
Prop: Reusable bag
Line: “All you need is one of these”
Requirements: 4-7 minutes long
the requirements also state that the team cannot hire, or spend money for locations, actors, crew, or equipment. and that the film must be made in a 48 hour time constraint.


the production team managed to get the studio 23 and Left Studios for free, allowing the use of building sets, props, lighting, and a guest apperance of the stanley hotel desk from the actual shining!
